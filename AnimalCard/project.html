Tiger is most recognisable for its dark vertical stripes on orange-brown fur with a lighter underside.

  • Scientific Name :Panthera tigris
  • Lifespan :10 – 15 years
  • Speed :49 – 65 km/h
  • Male Mass :90 – 310 kg

Tiger is an apex predator, primarily preying on ungulates such as deer and wild boar. It is territorial and generally a solitary but social predator, requiring large contiguous areas of habitat, which support its requirements for prey and rearing of its offspring.
